Our Philosophy

To understand and meet the needs of content providers while focussing on the end user experience.

Content providers need a solution that is compliant, cost effective, fits easily into current processes and doesn’t impact creative content designed to support their brand.

Screen reader and Braille display users need to access and navigate the same content that is available to visual users, using their choice of adaptive technology.

Accessibil-IT’s approach to document accessibility results in content providers having one document that can be leveraged to accommodate the wide technology choices available to end users to access digital content. We partner with our customers to ensure they achieve accessibility goals in the most cost effective manner, while sustaining the look and feel of their content design. We consult regularly with end users to ensure we continuously understand their needs, to better service our clients and their audience.
