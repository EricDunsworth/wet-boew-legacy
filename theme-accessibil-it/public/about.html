About Us

About Us

Accessibil-IT is an Oakville, Ontario based organization built to meet the unique accessibility needs of organizations around the world.

We are document accessibility specialists with a passion for excellence. Select About Us to learn more about what Accessibil-IT is about.

What We Believe

We believe in a culture that is driven by the opportunity to help our customers provide equal access to information for people with disabilities.

Accessibility can be achieved within today's fiscal realities. Select What We Believe to learn more about how we help meet both needs.

Partners

We specialize in content and document accessibility. It's all we do.

Partnering with industry leading accessibiity service providers allows us to recommend the best organizations to meet your other accessibility needs. Select Partners to learn more about our collaborative partners.

Clients

Accessibil-IT is committed to meeting the accessibility needs of all organizations from small and medium sized businesses to multi-nationals and governments.

Learn more about how Accessibil-IT is helping some of its clients to make their communications accessible by selecting Clients.
