Why Accessibil-IT

Accessibil-IT Inc. provides document accessibility services which are designed around a fully accessible platform allowing organizations to provide a single PDF document to all users. Our approach optimizes accessibility without changing the look or feel of the original content. Our approach protects brand design, and ensures the document can be fully accessed and navigated so it is properly understood by both visual and non-visual users.

Technical Ability

We have set a new standard in PDF accessibility with our technical capabilities. We go well beyond the basics of tagging, alt-text and tables, to ensure that users of adaptive technology have the same opportunity to access and navigate content as visual users. It is important to us that visual and non-visual users of your information have equal opportunity to understand your communications, working with the technology they choose.

How do we do this? We take the time to understand your communications and to ensure the reading order is accurate and the alt-text effectively conveys the content of images. We utilize our advanced knowledge of the PDF file format to ensure your content maintains its original look with added accessibility functionality from code to the presentation level. In addition, we consult regularly with users of adaptive technologies to stay current on technological advances and how they are utilized.

Fair Pricing

Fair pricing is a fundamental philosophy of Accessibil-IT. We recognize that for accessible communications to become part of day to day operations, our service must be cost effective. Our prices are based on the size and complexity of the document, not on the existence of a legislated requirement.

Commitment to Service

Accessibil-IT recognizes that important communications typically have processes to complete them and deadlines for issuance. We integrate seamlessly into your process, and can meet with your designers to provide guidance on structuring the document from an accessibility standpoint. We also dedicate sufficient resources to ensure deadlines are met.

Our commitment is to work alongside your team to meet your needs with limited added effort on your part.

Focus on the Needs of both Business and End Users

Accessibil-IT understands that document accessibility is only truly achieved when an end user has access to all of the information that the author intended to convey. Accessibil-IT prides itself on ensuring one document reaches the widest audience possible, cost effectively and without sacrificing message integrity or security.
