Intranet template for the Government of Canada

Who should use these templates

Any Government of Canada intranet can choose to use these templates. They are optional and are not required.

The motivation for the templates is to ensure that the intranet uses the same coding base as the internet, while giving it a distinct look with flexible and strategic design options. Refer to: Template principles

What are the perks?

Well aside from a ton of documentation, you get:

  • The ability to customize the look of the templates to meet your branding requirements
  • A style guide that can also be used with the internet code, as the they both share the same coding and styles.
  • An easy way to modernize your intranet.
  • You will be adhering to 2,400 year old math principles dealing with beauty and aethestics

Psst...don't overcomplicate it!

This tiny site consists of just a handful of pages and could have been easily been presented with a standard two column layout. However, a complex design is featured simply to showcase the versatility of the templates. Design elements like a site navigation bar and one column layouts are likely not needed on most intranets so don't perceive this exact design is the standard.
