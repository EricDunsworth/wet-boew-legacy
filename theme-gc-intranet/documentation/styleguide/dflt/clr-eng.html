Branding colours

Recommendation: Change to suit your Department's branding colours. The default colours are below:

White: #fff

Miscellaneous colours

Recommendation: Do not change. Some of these colours are integral to the template, while some are used occasionally. For example, the the purple (#5a306b) is the visited link colour, while the green (#e7efd6) is the background colour on module-summary and module-answer .
